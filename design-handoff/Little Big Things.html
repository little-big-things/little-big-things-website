01What we do

Three ways we’ll work together — or not at all.

We keep the practice small on purpose, because targeted, specific work produces outsized impact. Tim and Christina lead every engagement, and pull in the right collaborators when the work calls for them.

01 / Consulting

Data & AI strategyFor the team about to commit a year to it

We come in for the planning quarter, not the rebuild. Data strategy, AI strategy, and the business-development & marketing scaffolding that makes either one land. Output is a defensible plan, a working prototype, and the language your exec team will actually use in the next board meeting.

4–12 weeksFractional or fixed scope
02 / Investing

Early-stage checksWhere we’d have taken the meeting anyway

We write small checks into pre-seed and seed-stage companies building in our circle — AI infrastructure, vertical AI for under-served industries, and developer tooling. We invest where we can be useful for more than the cheque: intros, hiring loops, technical review.

$25k–$100kPre-seed / seed only
03 / Freelance

Data foundations & systemsStrategy, analytics, and the engineering that holds them up

The build that doesn’t fit a full hire. Data foundations — warehouses, pipelines, modelling. Analytics that an exec team will actually open. Systems improvement on what’s already shipped. We lead with our own hands, bring in trusted collaborators when the work calls for them, and ship code with the runbook to match.

2–8 weeksFixed-scope sprints

03Small, by design

Targeted work. Outsized impact.

Tim & Christina Frazer lead the practice. We stay small because specific, well-scoped work is where we move the needle — and we bring in trusted engineers and operators when an engagement calls for more hands.

Tim Frazer

Self-taught engineer turned multi-time Director of Data Engineering. Currently at Trust & Will, previously running data & analytics at Thrive Global (Arianna Huffington’s company) and a senior engineer at Bonobos through the Walmart acquisition.

Co-founder · Systems, ML & research

Christina Frazer

System Development Engineer at Amazon Web Services. PhD in Neurobiology from King’s College London, with a fellowship at Tohoku University on stroke imaging. JSPS Summer Fellow, George Brownlee Award.

Before AWS, launched and ran The Lancet Digital Health as a senior editor — the journal that ranked first in medical informatics in its first year of publication. Brings the research rigour to every engagement that touches health, life sciences, or evaluation methodology.
